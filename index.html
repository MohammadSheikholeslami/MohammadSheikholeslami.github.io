I'm a PhD candidate at the Department of Electrical and Computer engineering in University of Toronto. I received my B.Sc. degree in power electrical engineering from Bu-Ali Sina University, Iran, in 2017, and the M.Sc. degree in communication systemsengineering from the University of Tehran, Iran, in 2020.

At U of T I've worked on multi-modal federated learning over cell-free networks.
